How to Play
01

Flip two cards

Tap any two cards to reveal their icons. Matching pairs stay face-up; mismatches flip back after a moment.

02

Build your combo

Consecutive matches raise your combo multiplier for bonus points. A wrong guess resets it.

03

Watch your lives & timer

Each difficulty gives you a lives budget and a countdown. Run out of either and it's game over.

04

Use power-ups wisely

Hint, Freeze Timer, Extra Life, Reveal, Double Score and Shuffle can rescue a tough run — each has limited charges.

05

Earn XP & coins

Every win grants XP toward your player level and coins you can spend unlocking premium themes.

06

Clear the board to win

Match every pair before time or lives run out to earn a rank from C to S+, based on speed, moves and accuracy.

About
MISMATCH MAYHEM

A premium, single-file memory matching game built entirely with HTML5, CSS3 and vanilla JavaScript — no frameworks, no external assets. All icons are hand-built SVG, all sound is synthesized live with the Web Audio API, and every stat, achievement, XP level and coin is saved locally in your browser.
